Butter Chicken

Ingredients:
- 500g boneless chicken
- 1 cup yogurt
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tsp turmeric
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 2 tsp ground cumin
- 2 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1 tsp salt
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 1 can tomato puree
- 1 cup heavy cream
- Fresh cilantro for garnish

Biryani

Ingredients:
- 2 cups basmati rice
- 500g chicken, cut into pieces
- 1 large onion, thinly sliced
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 1 cup yogurt
- 2 tbsp biryani masala
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 4 cups water
- 3 tbsp oil or ghee
- Fresh cilantro and mint leaves for garnish
- Salt to taste

Paneer Tikka

Ingredients:
- 250g paneer, cut into cubes
- 1 cup yogurt
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1 tsp chaat masala
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 2 tbsp oil
- Salt to taste
- 1 large onion, cut into chunks
- 1 large bell pepper, cut into chunks
- Fresh cilantro for garnish
