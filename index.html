Butter Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:
- 500g boneless chicken
- 1 cup yogurt
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tsp turmeric
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 2 tsp ground cumin
- 2 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1 tsp salt
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 1 can tomato puree
- 1 cup heavy cream
- Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:
1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, lemon juice, turmeric, garam masala, cumin, chili powder, ground coriander, and salt. Let it sit for at least 1 hour.
2. Heat butter in a large pan over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and ginger, and sauté until onions are golden brown.
3. Add the marinated chicken and cook until the chicken is no longer pink.
4. Stir in the tomato puree and cook for another 10 minutes.
5. Add the heavy cream and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens.
6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.

Biryani

Ingredients:
- 2 cups basmati rice
- 500g chicken, cut into pieces
- 1 large onion, thinly sliced
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 1 cup yogurt
- 2 tbsp biryani masala
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 4 cups water
- 3 tbsp oil or ghee
- Fresh cilantro and mint leaves for garnish
- Salt to taste

Instructions:
1. Rinse the basmati rice in cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
2. Heat oil or ghee in a large pot over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
3. Add the sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
4. Add garlic and ginger, and sauté for another minute.
5. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink.
6. Stir in the chopped tomatoes, yogurt, biryani masala, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook for 10 minutes until the chicken is well coated with the spices.
7. Add the drained rice and 4 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and mint leaves before serving.

Paneer Tikka

Ingredients:
- 250g paneer, cut into cubes
- 1 cup yogurt
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1 tsp chaat masala
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 2 tbsp oil
- Salt to taste
- 1 large onion, cut into chunks
- 1 large bell pepper, cut into chunks
- Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:
1. In a large bowl, mix the yogurt, lemon juice, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, ground cumin, ground coriander, chaat masala, ginger-garlic paste, oil, and salt to make the marinade.
2. Add the paneer cubes, onion chunks, and bell pepper chunks to the marinade. Mix well to coat everything evenly. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour.
3. Preheat the oven to 200°C (392°F). Line a baking tray with aluminum foil and lightly grease it.
4. Arrange the marinated paneer, onion, and bell pepper on the baking tray. Bake for 15-20 minutes, turning halfway through, until the paneer is golden and slightly charred.
5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with mint chutney.
